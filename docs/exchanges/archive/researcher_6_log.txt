### New Publishable Findings

**Update-Rate Applicability Region**: Savings degrade linearly at ~3.75pp per 5% update rate. Break-even at p≈20% (low-σ tasks) to p≈30% (high-σ tasks). Produces a practitioner-ready "applicability region" figure.

**Cross-N Validity Condition**: The savings formula requires *uniform entity arrival across chunks* (not just large N). At N=100 (subsampled, front-loaded arrival), savings collapse due to high update rates — the same O(u·n) mechanism.

**Lazy Retraction Safety Condition** (`docs/lazy_retraction_analysis.md`): Monotone validity is the safety criterion. "Before DATE" tasks (1.3% bidirectional) are safe for lazy retraction; "After DATE" tasks (10.4% bidirectional) are not. Paper-and-pencil analysis complete.
