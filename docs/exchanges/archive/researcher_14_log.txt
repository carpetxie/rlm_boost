**2. No-Retraction Counterfactual (Critique #2 — zero cost)**
- Quantifies what happens WITHOUT retraction after entity edits:

| Metric | 5 Edits (With/Without) | 10 Edits (With/Without) |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Invalid pairs | 0 / **99** | 0 / **240** |
| Precision | **1.0** / 0.922 | **1.0** / 0.812 |

This proves retraction is **essential, not optional** — without it, precision drops to 0.81.
